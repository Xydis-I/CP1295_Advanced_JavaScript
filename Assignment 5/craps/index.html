Street craps

Rules:

  • If you roll 7 or 11 on the first (come out) roll, you win.
  • If you roll 2, 3, or 12 on the come out roll, you lose.
  • Any other number on the come out roll sets the point.
  • If you roll the point before you roll 7, you win.
  • If you roll 7 before you roll the point, you lose.
